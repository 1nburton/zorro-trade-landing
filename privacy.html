Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Zorro Trade ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number — used solely for account authentication via SMS one-time passcode (OTP). We do not sell or share your phone number.
  • Billing email — collected when you subscribe to Zorro Trade Pro, used to associate your Stripe subscription with your account.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics such as screen views and feature interactions, used to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your session.
  • To process and manage your subscription payment via Stripe.
  • To display personalized stock screening results and market data.
  • To send account-related notifications (e.g., subscription status changes).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — phone number authentication and SMS delivery.
  • Stripe — payment processing. We never store your full card details.
  • Supabase — secure database storage for account and subscription records.
  • Finnhub — real-time and historical stock market data.
  • Yahoo Finance — supplemental live price data.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Zorro Trade is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and row-level security on our database to protect your data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
